What is PDR?

Paintless Dent Repair (PDR) is the fastest, most affordable way to repair vehicle body damage without the need for traditional body shop methods (bondo, sanding, or repainting).

Common Repairs Include:

  • Door Dings & Shopping Cart Dents
  • Sharp Creases in Fenders & Doors
  • Hail Damage Repair
  • Minor Plastic Bumper Indentations
  • Hood & Roof Dents

Why Choose Mobile PDR?

  • Repairs often take hours, not days.
  • No risk of paint color mismatch.
  • Maintains vehicle resale value.
